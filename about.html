About Me

Hello! I'm Abir, a passionate and creative web designer from Bangladesh. I specialize in building elegant, responsive websites using just HTML and CSS — no frameworks. I believe in keeping things lightweight yet beautiful.

I’m currently working on my personal portfolio project to showcase my growing skills in front-end development and constantly learning and improving.

Beyond coding, I love watching cricket and football. I enjoy working on futuristic AI ideas, like building an AI or making search engines. I’m also interested in deep tech, and I dream of building something truly intelligent.

I wear glasses, have long hair, and care deeply about making my work stand out. Whether it’s experimenting with new design ideas or writing code late into the night, I always bring passion and personality into what I create.
